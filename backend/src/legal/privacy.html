Privacy Policy

PHD — Pet Health Director · Last updated: March 2026

Overview

PHD ("we", "our", "the app") is a pet food analysis tool that helps pet owners make informed decisions about their pet's nutrition. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Information We Collect

  • Device identifier: A random, anonymous identifier generated by your device. We use this solely to associate your pet profiles and scan history with your device. We do not collect your name, email, phone number, or Apple ID.
  • Pet profiles: Information you voluntarily provide about your pets (name, type, breed, age, weight, health conditions). This is stored to personalize food analysis results.
  • Scan data: Photos of pet food labels are processed in real-time to extract ingredient lists. Images are not stored on our servers — only the extracted text and analysis results are retained.
  • Usage data: Scan counts and feature usage to provide badge/gamification features. No personal analytics or tracking SDKs are used.

How We Use Your Information

  • To analyze pet food ingredients and provide personalized safety scores.
  • To cache analysis results so repeated scans of the same product are faster.
  • To sync your pet profiles across sessions on the same device.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. Ingredient data may be sent to Google's Gemini AI service for analysis — this data contains only ingredient text, not any personal or device information.

Data Storage & Security

Data is stored on secure servers (Railway cloud infrastructure). Pet profiles are also cached locally on your device. All network communication uses HTTPS encryption.

Data Retention & Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time by using the "Reset App" feature in Settings. This removes your pet profiles and scan history from both your device and our servers.

Children's Privacy

PHD does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is intended for pet owners of all ages and does not require any personal information to function.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
